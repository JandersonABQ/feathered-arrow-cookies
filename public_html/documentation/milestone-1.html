PWP Milestone 1

Purpose, Audience & Goal

Purpose

For this project I will be using front-end web development to create a website for a company brochure.

Audience

This site will be designed with the consumer in mind, and is meant to showcase the company's customizable products. The target audience will be consumers who are celebrating all occasions.

Goal

The goal of this website is to advertise company products and establish a way to interact with customers. I will also use this site to develop my skills in web development.

Persona

Name:

Raquel Garza

Age:

29

Profession:

Raquel is the owner of Feathered Arrow Cookies, a custom cookie business that helps customers celebrate all occasions.

Technology:

Apple power user. uses Macbook for emailing customers, keeping up with social media presence, editing videos and pictures. Raquel uses her Iphone 13 for taking pictures, recording videos, and applications for customer payments. Raquel also uses an Ipad with digital media to create cookies ideas before making the actual cookies.

Attitudes and Behaviors:

Raquel is the sole operator for her business and wants to find ways to streamline the order taking process and customer interaction. Raquel wants a way to promote her business and create a professional online presence. Her goal is to use the website to market her business without spending excessive time on social media platforms.

Frustrations and Needs:

Raquel currently uses social media to promote her business and finds it hard to manage multiple platforms for communicating with her customers. Raquel wants to separate her business operations from the social media apps and direct customers to her website, so she can easily take orders and schedule them appropriately. one problem Raquel had been having was double booking or sometimes losing orders through her current process.

Goals:

Raquel wants to simplify the business process on her side and for the customers. User Story: As a user I want to reduce my business operations and focus on baking cookies!
