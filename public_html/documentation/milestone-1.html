PWP Milestone 1

Purpose, Audience & Goal

Purpose

For this project I will be using front-end web development to create a website for a
company brochure.

Audience

This site will be designed with the consumer in mind, and is meant to showcase the
company's customizable products. The target audience will be consumers who are purchasing cookies
to celebrating all types of occasions.

Goal

My goal for this website is to build a site that advertises company products and establishes
a way to interact with customers. I want the site to highlight the custom and creative aspect of the
business by showcasing the different cookies. I want to use subtle colors and layouts so the cookies
will draw the focus of the audience. My other goal is to continue to expound on my skills in web
development and further my knowledge in the front-end fundamentals.

Persona

Name:

Amber

Age:

29

Profession:

Amber is a Human Resource representative for a private health care company.

Technology:

Amber is a power user of technology. she uses computers and office equipment every day for her
Job. She uses Microsoft applications like office, word, and excel. Amber also uses Zoom and other
office applications to interact with her associates. In Amber's personal life she uses Apple
products like Macbook, iphone, and Apple Watch. Amber loves having all the new technology and
because she believes it makes her life easier.

Attitudes and Behaviors:

As a Human Resource rep Amber has to plan events and work celebrations. Not all
celebrations are the same, so she needs ways to make them more specific to the festivity.
Amber is looking for a bakery that can make deserts that fit themes of work ceremonies like
employee birthdays, anniversaries, holidays, and retirements.

Frustrations and Needs:

Amber likes to plan events in advance and needs a company that takes orders early
and will have her order ready on the set day.
Amber finds the desserts at local grocery stores
to always be the same, and she wants different products that can be made for the individual that
is being celebrated (favorite sports team, superhero, or other personal interests).
Amber has turned to local bakeries to find desserts but many of them
don't have websites
to view products or place orders.
Amber is looking for a local baker who can provide
an easy-to-use ordering system that does not require her to order in person, which takes
away from her being in the office.
Amber is having trouble finding businesses that have both custom products and have an online
presence to view products and have the ability to place orders.

Goals:

I want to find a bakery website that makes custom orders, has prices and pictures of their
work, so I don't have to place orders in person.

User Story:

I want to make it easier to celebrate my coworkers.
