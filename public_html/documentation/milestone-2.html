Content Strategy

This company brochure will be one page and will consist of the following sections:

Navbar:

I want the website to have a navbar at the top with the company logo(feathered arrow cookies). I want the navbar to contain links for navigation to future pages that I will make after bootcamp. the navbar links will be home, about us, gallery, services(other desserts), and contact us.

Jumbotron:

I want to display a jumbotron with as a welcome section. I want this section to have some text and an order now button. I want the text to say "custom cookies for all occasions." this section will also have so cookie creations.

Container:

In this section I want to have an area of text to separate the jumbotron and the other picture gallery. this section will have say "let us help you celebrate life's moments".

Picture collection:

This section will have a picture gallery to show what cookies she has made and for what occasions. (birthdays, weddings, baby showers, holidays, and retirements). I want to make the cookie pictures enlarge when the customer hovers over them.

Container:

This section will have some text and a button for the cookie collections link that will be added after bootcamp. the text in this section will say "Need some inspiration for your event?..check out our cookie collections". I have added this section to split the gallery and the about me section, so the webpage is not flooded with photos and has areas to break up photo sections.

About Me:

I want to include an about me section with a picture of the owner. I also want to include a quick bio of how she got started, types of cookie designs, and info about her business.

Reviews:

I want to add a section of customer reviews. I want to display her business rating with a stars(point system). and display some customer comments. I want to also have an area to leave a review.

Contact Info Section:

I want to have a section for customers to contact the owner. this section will have email link, FAQ link, and section for following on social media sites. I want this section to have a heading that says "Connect with us" and have logos for facebook, instagram, and TikTok.

Footer:

And last I want a footer with the same links in the top navbar so customers can navigate without having to scroll back to the top.
